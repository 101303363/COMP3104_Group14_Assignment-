- George Brown College is a public college with three campuses in downtown Toronto, it was chartered in 1966 by the government of Ontario
  and opened the next year

- GBC is known to be the first college to start a distance education system

- Casa Loma campus is located at 160 Kendal Ave, Toronto.
  -> it is considered the heart of information technology courses offered by GBC
  -> for more information about this campus please visit https://www.georgebrown.ca/about/campuses-locations/casa-loma
